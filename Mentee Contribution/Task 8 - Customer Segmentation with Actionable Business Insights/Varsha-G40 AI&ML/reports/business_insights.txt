BUSINESS INSIGHTS

1. High Value Customers

• High Monetary Value
• High Purchase Frequency
• High Income

Recommendation:
Offer premium membership and exclusive rewards.

--------------------------------------------------

2. Loyal Customers

• Purchase regularly
• Good spending habits

Recommendation:
Provide loyalty points and personalized offers.

--------------------------------------------------

3. New Customers

• Low purchase history
• Recent customers

Recommendation:
Offer welcome discounts and onboarding campaigns.

--------------------------------------------------

4. At-Risk Customers

• High Recency
• Low Frequency

Recommendation:
Run re-engagement campaigns with personalized emails.

--------------------------------------------------

5. Budget Customers

• Low income
• Low spending

Recommendation:
Offer discounts and bundle products.

--------------------------------------------------

Overall Business Recommendation

Increase marketing investment on High Value and Loyal Customers.

Reduce customer churn using personalized campaigns.

Use RFM segmentation for targeted marketing.
